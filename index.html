Introduction

Here's an introduction of a media magnate with a persona doughed with courage, boldness, resilience, and determination. This is about a renowned Indian journalist who is also a writer, thinker, motivator, and orator. As a Journalist & Editor-In-Chief making a difference in the neo-Indian media industry with an impactful endeavor through his media house, 'The Printlines Media Group', Mr. Upendra Rai is Chairman & Managing Director, and Editor-in-Chief of Bharat Express News Network. Earlier he rendered his valuable services as CEO and Editor-in-Chief forSahara India Media and Tehelka.Mr. Rai embarked upon the journalistic journey with strong determinationand thrust towards uncovering the truth.

He was associated with variousesteemed media organizations, such as Sahara News Network, Star News, CNBC-Awaz, and Tehelka; elevating his dynamic skills where gradually hedeveloped a reputation for his investigative reporting. Mr. Rai delved deepinto successfully associating with various key subjects, unearthingcorruption, scandals, and financial irregularities hogging media highlightsthat captured the nation's attention during his escalating journey.Standing tall with his own media outlet, the Editor-in-Chief at BharatExpress, Mr. Rai displays exceptional leadership skills along with a firmcommitment to journalistic integrity. Under his apex supervision andguidance, the News network flourished to new heights, providing a platformfor honest, responsible, and realistic voices delivering impactful stories forthe readers.

Mr. Rai's far-sighted vision for Bharat Express presents animpeccable dream for all journalists to realize, creating a platform thatinforms, stimulates meaningful conversations, and promotes responsiblejournalism in new India.Witnessing the voyage as a vocal and fearless journalist, and facingmanifold challenges on personal and professional fronts, Mr. Rai continueswith a firm resolve to inspire and to contribute his life with determinedefforts towards the pursuit of truth and transparency.
